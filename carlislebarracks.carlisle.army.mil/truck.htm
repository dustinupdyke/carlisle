Commercial Truck Access

All trucks 5-ton or larger must use the dedicated truck inspection point at the Claremont Gate Access Control Point prior to entering the post. Trucks will be subject to a vehicle inspection. Drivers to include all passengers must submit Carlisle Barracks Form 228, Installation Access Application and will undergo a background check before being issued an Installation Access Control Pass. Once approved, passes will be available at the Claremont Gate Access Control Point. If you will need access exceeding 30 days you will need to have your Contracting Officer Representative (COR) or Government Employee Sponsor (GES) sign off on your Installation Access Control Application.

How to Submit Carlisle Barracks Form 228, Installation Access Application

There are three methods you can use to submit Carlisle Barracks Form 228, Installation Access Application. You can submit your application up to 30 days in advance.

  1. Bring your completed Carlisle Barracks Form 228 with you to the Claremont Gate Access Control Point.
